A Collection of 4 library cards.


Fairly recently, I've made a hobby out of collecting library cards.
This comes from the discovery that you can obtain the card of up to 
18 different libraries if you're a Vancouver resident. (The system
for that is called Interlink if you're curious, and I like
their website because they make it sound like a political alliance).

Anyways, so far I have gained 3 5! library cards:

    

  1. Vancouver/VPL:
    I live in Vancouver, making this pretty straightforward.
    2. 

  2. North Vancouver City/NVCL
    Got this one while walking with a friend. This was also
    how I found out about the existence of multiple North Vancouver's.
    3. 

  3. Burnaby/BPL
    One day I had 2.5hrs between things I had to go to, so I made
    a quick trip to the neighbouring city. Good times.
    4. 

  4. Coquitlam/CPL
    During an in-person hackathon, I decided to go take a break and went to Coquitlam.
    5. 

  5. Port Moody/PMPL
    On the way back from going to Coquitlam, (see above) I made a stop at Port Moody.
    6. 


Technically, I have two Vancouver library cards, the old one and the new
one, but that doesn't truly count in my opinion.
